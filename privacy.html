Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 April 2026

1. Who we are

ACSO Consulting LTD (EIK 201054736), based in Sofia, Bulgaria, operates the website acsoconsulting.com. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this policy.

Contact: info@acsoconsulting.com

2. What data we collect

We collect data only through two channels:

a) Contact form submissions

  • Name
  • Work email address
  • Company name
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Free-text context about your business needs

This data is submitted voluntarily by you. We use it solely to respond to your inquiry and to evaluate whether our services are a fit.

b) Analytics cookies (with your consent)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Microsoft Clarity to understand how visitors interact with the site. These tools collect anonymised usage data such as pages viewed, scroll depth, click patterns, and referral source. Clarity additionally records anonymised session replays and heatmaps to help us improve the user experience. Sensitive form inputs are automatically masked.

3. Legal basis for processing

  • Contact form data: Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — we process your inquiry to respond to your request for information about our services.
  • Analytics cookies: Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR) — only activated after you grant explicit consent via the cookie banner.

4. How we use your data

  • To respond to your inquiry within one business day
  • To evaluate whether our services are relevant to your needs
  • To understand aggregate site usage patterns (analytics, consent-dependent)

We do not use your data for automated decision-making, profiling, or advertising.

5. Who receives your data

  • Google LLC (analytics processor, under EU Standard Contractual Clauses) — only if you consent to cookies
  • Microsoft Corporation (Clarity session analytics, under EU Standard Contractual Clauses) — only if you consent to cookies
  • Hosting provider — our web server processes form submissions; no data is shared beyond what is needed for email delivery

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

6. Data retention

  • Contact form data: Retained for up to 12 months after the last communication, then deleted.
  • Analytics data: Retained for 14 months within Google Analytics, then automatically deleted.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for analytics at any time (clear your cookies or click "Reject" on the banner)
  • Lodge a complaint with the Bulgarian Commission for Personal Data Protection (CPDP) or your local supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, email info@acsoconsulting.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data security

We protect your data through HTTPS encryption in transit, server-side input validation, rate limiting, and restricted access to form submission records. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures appropriate to the sensitivity of the data involved.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be noted on the main website.
