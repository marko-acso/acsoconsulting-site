Privacy Policy

Last updated: 10 April 2026

Who we are

ACSO Consulting LTD (EIK 201054736), Sofia, Bulgaria ("we", "us") operates the website acsoconsulting.com. We provide B2B business development services.

What data we collect

We collect only what you provide directly or what is necessary for the site to function:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, company, phone number (optional), and any context you provide. This data is sent via email to our team and is not stored in a database.
  • Analytics data: if you accept cookies, Google Analytics 4 (GA4) collects anonymized usage data such as pages visited, time on site, and approximate location (country/city level). This data is processed by Google under their privacy policy.

Cookies

We use one first-party cookie:

  • cookie_consent — records your cookie preference (accepted or declined). Expires after 12 months.

If you accept analytics cookies, Google Analytics sets additional cookies (_ga, _ga_*) to distinguish unique visitors. These are only loaded after your explicit consent. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and no usage data is collected.

How we use your data

  • Contact form data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and to follow up if appropriate.
  • Analytics data helps us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data retention

Contact form emails are retained in our inbox as part of normal business correspondence. Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months (Google's default), after which it is automatically deleted.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can also withdraw consent for analytics cookies at any time by clearing your browser cookies — the consent banner will reappear on your next visit.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@acsoconsulting.com.
